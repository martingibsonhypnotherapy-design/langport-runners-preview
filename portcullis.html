A race worth getting muddy for

From the river
to the Levels.

Start on Cocklemoor, then head out across the beautiful countryside surrounding Langport.

The Portcullis Trail 10K follows mixed-terrain trails through the Levels, local droves and rolling countryside, with scenery and wide Somerset views throughout. Every finisher receives a medal.

Know the course

Trail ready.

01

Clearly marked

Course signage and a team of marshals will guide runners around the route.

02

Water at 5K

A water station is positioned around halfway. This is a cupless event, so bring your own cup.

03

Medical support

Medical cover is available at the start and finish area and along the course.

04

Race rules

No headphones and no running with dogs. The event follows UKA rules.
